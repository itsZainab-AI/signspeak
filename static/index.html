00 About this assistant

SignSpeak is a Generative AI-powered accessibility assistant built for the FIFA World Cup 2026 Smart Stadiums challenge, addressing the Accessibility and Multilingual Assistance pillars.

  • Scan & translate — OCR reads any sign or notice, then translates it via MyMemory and generates a plain-language explanation using Gemma 2 (via HuggingFace).
  • Ask a question — the AI answers venue questions in natural language, drawn from a stadium FAQ knowledge base.
  • Accessible & free — built with open-source tools — Tesseract OCR, MyMemory translation, and HuggingFace inference — no paid APIs required.

Powered by Tesseract OCR · MyMemory · HuggingFace · Built for FIFA World Cup 2026
